Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a pretty good dish. I'm a fan of it. It's a shame it's so calorie dense otherwise I'd enjoy it a lot more often than I currently do.

If I enjoyed it as much as I'd like without concern for the calories I'd probably end up just like Garfield.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Meat
  • Seasonings
  1. Cook the meat
  2. Combine the meat with the tomato sauce
  3. Boil the pasta
  4. Assemble the meat/tomato juice mixture, cheese, and cooked pasta into the lasagna
  5. Bake for an appropriate length of time
  6. Enjoy with abandon
